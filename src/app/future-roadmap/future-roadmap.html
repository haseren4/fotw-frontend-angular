Roadmap

  • Army posts only; directory, live map, basic logger, ADIF export
  • Safety & compliance guidance; FOTA UI & icon set

Next · Q1–Q2 2026

  • Awards & stats, offline logging, spots in/out, QRZ lookup
  • LoTW/Club Log export (phase 1); map overlays; community guides

Later · Q3–Q4 2026

  • Guard/Reserve + 1 additional branch (pilot); photos & AAR
  • PWA offline directory/tiles; public read API v1

Future · 2027+

  • Global forts & multilingual UI; advanced awards; data partnerships
