Getting Started

Fortifications on the Air (FOTA) is a portable-radio activity celebrating current and historic sites. Current phase: activations are limited to Active Duty U.S. Army locations. If your location is not listed, please request a new location with all data. There are several locations that are missing location data, so please help us out!

Quick Start

  1. Pick a site on the map
  2. Plan your activation
  3. Get on the air & upload your log

Gear Checklist

  • Portable rig + antenna
  • Battery / approved power
  • Coax, adapters, throw line
  • Logging (paper or app)
  • ID, water, sun/bug protection

Operating Guidelines

  • Respect the site & visitors
  • Follow RF safety and local rules
  • Use minimum necessary power
  • “CQ FOTA” + callsign, spot if allowed
  • Leave no trace

Operate from publicly accessible areas designated on the site page. Stay clear of restricted zones.

Any amateur mode/band permitted by your license and local policy. Check site notes for RF-sensitive areas.

Only during posted open hours unless otherwise stated by the site.
