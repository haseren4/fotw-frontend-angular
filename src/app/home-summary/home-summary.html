What is FOTA?

Fortifications on the Air (FOTA) encourages licensed amateurs to operate from or near documented fortifications—medieval castles, trace-italienne star forts, coastal batteries, redoubts, and earthworks. Every activation adds to a living log of radio activity tied to history and geography.

For Activators

  1. Pick a fort and confirm access/permissions.
  2. Activate on-site with safe, low-impact portable ops.
  3. Log contacts; your activation updates in real time.

For Chasers

  1. Watch the live map for on-air or scheduled activations.
  2. Make the contact on the posted frequency/mode.
  3. Claim credit and track your awards.
